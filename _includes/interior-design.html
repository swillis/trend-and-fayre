Interior Design

Trend and Fayre offers a low-key interior design service that is totally flexible to your needs. I love real homes and believe that decorating should be fun – there are no specific rules or formulas to follow. I hope to help you create inspiring and comfortable spaces that really reflect your lifestyle and personality. You won’t get any matchy matchy, cold hotel vibe here!

I like to keep this service really personal. I am happy to help you with anything, from a simple consultation to bounce some fresh ideas around, to selecting new furniture or giving some life to your old pieces by re-upholstering and painting, right the way through to a full re-design and refurbishment of your whole property. It’s completely up to you!

So whatever your needs, Trend and Fayre is here to support, advise and encourage you through every step of your re-design.

If you are interested in the service, please feel free to get in touch and see how l can help you out.
